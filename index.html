Annular Capacity

Hole ID & Pipe OD → L/m (annulus)

Annular capacity: – L/m

Formula: L/m = π × (Dhole² − Dpipe²) / 4000

Pipe Capacity

Pipe ID → L/m (inside pipe)

Pipe capacity: – L/m

Formula: L/m = π × ID² / 4000

Bottoms-Up Time

Annular cap, interval length & pump rate → minutes

Bottoms-up time: – minutes

Volume (L) = cap × length  •  Time (min) = Volume / Rate
